How Environmental Temperature Influences Stress Levels and the Decision to Play The Sims 4

Hypothesis: I hypothesize that environmental temperature influences my decision to play The Sims 4, with longer gaming sessions occurring on colder days. Furthermore, I expect that the temperature, combined with these gameplay sessions, significantly influences my stress reduction.

Why this is one of my best plots:This visualization is the most effective way to represent my findings because it highlights how temperature acts as the primary driver for both my stress levels and my subsequent decision to play. By placing Temperature on the Y-axis and Minutes Played on the X-axis, the plot clearly illustrates the correlation between environmental conditions and behavioral choices. The use of categorical circle sizes for stress (Relax, Tense, and Very Stressed) allows for an immediate understanding of how my emotional state shifts under different thermal conditions.
