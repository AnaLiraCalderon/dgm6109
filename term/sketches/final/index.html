Three Visualization Proposals

This research focuses on the intersection of digital entertainment and environmental conditions. Specifically, I am investigating how my interaction with The Sims 4 functions as a stress-regulation mechanism. The data points collected for this study are defined by the following metrics:

  • Playtime: Duration of the gaming session in minutes (Estimated range: 0–60).
  • Final Stress Level: My perceived level of tension after playing (Scale: 0–5).
  • Daily Temperature: Categorical data (Cold Days (>=-1°C), Moderate (=10°C), Hot Days (=16°C)).
  • Time of Day: Categorical data (Morning (10:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m.), Afternoon (12:00 p.m. - 5:00 p.m.), Evening (5:00 p.m. - 8:00 p.m.)).
