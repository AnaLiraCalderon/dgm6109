HANOI DRIP COFEE

Description

Bringing the heart of Vietnam to Vancouver, this café specializes in authentic, sustainably sourced Robusta coffee. Known for being bold, smooth, and twice as strong as Arabica, their brews are designed to fuel the day. Visitors are invited to discover signature Phin drips, including their famous Egg Coffee and vibrant Ube Coconut lattes.
