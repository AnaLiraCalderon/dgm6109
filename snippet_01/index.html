Attractive lagoon Chinancocha (Llanganuco)

Mirrors of the Sky

A living legend

The foot of the snow-capped Huascarán, in the Llanganuco ravine, nature offers one of the best spectacles that humans can appreciate. If they were two mirrors of the sky, the Chinancocha and Orconcocha lagoons have created a new color in nature. They are not green, nor blue. Maybe they resemble turquoise. The only certainty is that its beauty leaves visitors speechless: travelers are perplexed by an incontrovertible example of how beautiful Mother Earth can be.
